🔍 Step 1: Search for Products (User Action)

Mulebuy supports multiple ways to find the items you love:

1. Browse Chinese e-commerce platforms such as Taobao, Weidian, 1688, etc., copy the link of the item you wish to purchase, and paste it into Mulebuy's search bar.

2. Enter keywords of the product name directly into Mulebuy's search bar to browse search results from platforms like Taobao, 1688, Weidian, and more.

3. You can also search by image: go to the Mulebuy homepage, click the camera icon on the right side of the search bar, select a saved product image, and browse search results for similar items.

🛒 Step 2: Select Products and Complete Payment (User Action)

Choose specifications (size, color, etc.), confirm the shipping address, and complete the payment for the items. International shipping costs will be calculated separately later. After your order is submitted, our purchasing agents will begin processing it. If you have special requests, you can add notes during checkout or contact customer service—the Mulebuy team will handle them for you.

📦 Step 3: Order Acceptance and Domestic Transit (Mulebuy Action)

Once your order is accepted, the items will be shipped from the seller to Mulebuy's transit warehouse in China. You can track the domestic logistics progress in real time from your [Orders] list.

✅ Step 4: Quality Check and Warehousing (Mulebuy Action)

When items arrive at the warehouse, they undergo a strict quality inspection. Once approved, they are safely stored and displayed in your [Warehouse] list, ready for international shipping submission.

🛫 Step 5: Submit International Shipping (User Action)

Go to your [Warehouse], select the items you want to ship, click "Pre-calculate" or "Submit," choose your preferred packing method and international shipping line, and confirm to submit your parcel for international delivery. Mulebuy also offers additional parcel services such as package removal, reinforced packaging, etc., which you can add during the process — the Mulebuy team will handle them. The actual international shipping cost will be calculated based on the parcel's true size and weight, with any difference refunded or charged accordingly — no need to worry.

📮 Step 6: Track Logistics and Share for Rewards (User Action)

You can check the logistics status of your parcel in the [Parcels] section. After successful delivery, you can share your shopping experience in the [Rewards] section to earn cash rewards.

If you encounter any issues while using Mulebuy, please feel free to contact our customer service team. We are committed to actively addressing any problems or concerns you may have.
