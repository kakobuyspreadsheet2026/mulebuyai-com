Can my order be refunded?

1. Order status “Pending”: you can cancel in User Center → Orders. Payment is refunded to your Mulebuy balance immediately.

2. Order status “Processing”: you can cancel in User Center → Orders. If we have not purchased the items yet, we cancel and refund your Mulebuy balance within one business day.

3. Order status “Purchased”: open User Center → Orders and tap After-sales on the order. We ask the seller for a refund. If the seller has not shipped, after seller confirmation the amount is returned to your Mulebuy balance—usually within about 3 days.

4. Order status “Seller shipped”: if the seller uses a fake tracking number or never ships, open User Center → Orders → After-sales. We request a refund from the seller; after confirmation, funds return to your Mulebuy balance—usually within about 7 days.

5. Five days after status becomes “Stored in warehouse”: you may open User Center → Orders → After-sales to request a return with the seller; approval is not guaranteed. If approved, we process the return as quickly as possible and the return tracking appears on the order. After the seller receives the goods and refunds, the system credits your Mulebuy balance—typically 7–15 days depending on how quickly the seller confirms.
